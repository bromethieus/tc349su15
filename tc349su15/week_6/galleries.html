American Hornbeam
A good tree for beginners as it hardy to both root and foliage pruning and grow in a variety of environments provided it is watered regularly.

Juniper
Juniper can be rewarding trees if properly cared for. It is still suitable for beginners with a space with plenty of morning sun and can water the tree in the morning to assure proper growth.

Trident Maple
Trident Maples are highly desired due to their small leaves and thick gnarly roots. They prefer full sun and will be most vibrant and successful grown in those conditions.
